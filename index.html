What is real estate drone photography?

Drone photography in real estate requires unmanned aerial vehicles equipped with high-resolution cameras to capture aerial images and videos of properties, offering comprehensive and engaging visuals.

Why you should use real estate drone photography

Drones offer enhanced property visualization, competitive marketing advantages, and increased online engagement through striking aerial perspectives that appeal to potential buyers.

Getting started with drone photography

To start, consider licensing requirements, drone types, and pricing. Choose a drone based on your needs and comply with all aviation regulations if flying commercially.

Hiring a professional drone operator

Look for certified, insured professionals with quality equipment and a strong portfolio. Discuss pricing, deliverables, and revision policies before hiring.

Top tips for real estate drone photography

Plan your shots, use ideal lighting, follow a stable flight path, ensure legal compliance, adjust camera settings, and enhance media with post-processing.

How Luxury Presence uses drone photography and videography
